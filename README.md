# Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates the design and implementation of a business intelligence dashboard for an e-commerce company. The goal was to analyze sales, profit margins, top-performing products, and customer segmentation.

## Key Features
- Interactive visuals with slicers for Region, Date, and Customer Segment
- Revenue and Profit analysis with calculated DAX measures
- Product and Category insights for strategic business decisions
- Clean, executive-ready dashboard suitable for presentations

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Excel (dataset preparation)
- DAX for calculated columns and measures

## Insights
- The East region generates the highest revenue, but the West has better profit margins
- Laptops and Phones are the most profitable product categories
- Corporate and Business segments contribute significantly to revenue but have varying profit margins

## Portfolio Note
This dashboard demonstrates the ability to turn raw sales data into actionable business insights, with clear visualization and executive storytelling.
